HAZMAT / EV FIRE PROTOCOL

⚠️ Explosive thermal runaway or chemical leak risk.
  1. Move at least 100 meters upwind immediately.
  2. Do NOT attempt to extinguish EV battery fires with water.
  3. Do NOT inhale fumes — cover nose and mouth.
  4. Keep bystanders far back. Block the road if safe to do so.
  5. Call fire brigade — specify HAZMAT or EV fire.
📞 Call Fire (101)

MASS CASUALTY MODE

Multiple-victim incident detected. Multi-ambulance dispatch flagged.
Field triage — counterintuitive but lifesaving:
  1. 🚫 IGNORE victims who are screaming or walking — they can wait.
  2. 🔍 LOCATE victims who are silent or breathing heavily — check them first.
  3. 🩸 Control catastrophic bleeding with direct pressure or improvised tourniquet.
  4. 🛣 Clear the road if safe — prevent secondary pile-ups.
  5. 📞 Tell dispatcher: "MCI — multiple victims" so they send more units.
📞 Call 108
